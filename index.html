Google + Alfred

I love Alfred. I also love google. I use them both, a lot. I’ll often type my google search queries directly into alfred, which then opens up a chrome tab with the search results.

But often times I don’t use google to search for websites, I use it too look up information. Information like APPL’s stock, or conversion rate between USD and EURO, or to translate “I think I love you” into spanish. Wouldn’t it be great if we could see these results directly from Alfred? I made a quick mockup to show what that would look like.
